About Us

We are ViGuedenrus Defeater, an innovation team dedicated to eliminating hidden mosquito breeding grounds—such as stagnant water and clogged drainage—that fuel the spread of dengue in the Jaro District. To combat this public health challenge, we developed a mobile application designed to detect local breeding hotspots, rapidly spread community awareness, and provide actionable prevention strategies. Our initiative aligns with the proactive containment guidelines enforced by the Iloilo City Health Office (CHO), which actively monitors vulnerable residential hubs in Jaro to prevent seasonal transmission surges.

    Our Ultimate Goal:
    At the end of the day, real innovation isn’t just about having a cool app on a screen—it’s about keeping people safe out in the real world. With MosqDetecto, our team wants to change how we deal with seasonal dengue outbreaks here in Iloilo City. Instead of just reacting with fear when people start getting sick, we want to give everyone the foresight and tools to step up early. The fight against dengue doesn’t start in a hospital; it starts right in our own backyards, handles through our data, and succeeds when we all look out for one another.
